Our COVID-19 Medical Supply Guide is a comprehensive guide to understanding which medical supplies and devices are used during the treatment of a COVID-19 patient, as well as information on global needs for these products.

It is a resource specifically for makers and contains the information they need to produce supplies and devices required during this global pandemic.

About OSMS

We are an organization coordinating relief efforts in response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Open Source Medical Supplies helps local fabricators and institutions responsibly create and distribute protective gear and medical equipment to communities in crisis.

We provide medically and technically reviewed open source design plans to fabricators of all scales, and offer guidance to community leaders to help organize effective local response efforts.

Goal 1

Consolidate reviewed designs that can be immediately produced.

Goal 2

Educate and connect volunteers together.

Goal 3

Offer support, mentorship, and guidance to local communities.
